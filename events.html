NMGS UNILAG Presents: How To Beat The System

Date: 3rd February, 2024
Time: 2PM
Our esteemed panelists:
Francis Olukomaya:Valedictorian of the 2021/2022 academic session, Francis will share his insights on academic excellence and how he consistently achieved top grades.
Nwachokor Otito: A master of balancing academics and extracurricular activities, Nwachokor will discuss his time management techniques and how he excelled in both areas.
Ruby Efetobore: An inspiration to all, Ruby will share her journey of overcoming academic challenges and developing effective study habits to achieve a strong GPA.

This is a great chance to learn how to balance schoolwork with other stuff you do, and how to overcome challenges and develop strong study habits. It's perfect for anyone in geoscience, no matter what year you're in, or even if you just want to learn better study skills in general. Don't miss out – come learn from the best!

400 level Geoscience students at UNILAG, get ready to land that internship!

Anuola Osinaike, a Reservoir Engineer at Sahara Group and alumni of your department, will be hosting an AMA (Ask Me Anything) session specifically for you. Anuola knows what it takes to succeed in geoscience. During his time at UNILAG, he led the student organization, then known as the University of Lagos Geosciences Student Society (ULGSS), which is now NMGS UNILAG. This AMA is your chance to:
  • Learn from Anuola's experience as he shares his top tips for crafting winning internship applications.
  • Ask him anything related to the internship process - no question is off limits!
  • Gain valuable insights from a successful alumni who has been in your shoes.
Don't miss this opportunity to get ahead in your geoscience career path! Mark your calendars for 7th December, 2023 and get ready to ask Anuola anything!
